about oculoplastic surgery

Oculoplastic surgery is a specialized branch of ophthalmology that focuses on the diagnosis and surgical treatment of conditions affecting the structures around the eyes, including the eyelids, tear ducts, and the orbit (eye socket). It addresses both functional issues, such as drooping eyelids (ptosis), tear duct blockages, and orbital fractures, as well as cosmetic concerns like eyelid reshaping and wrinkle reduction. Oculoplastic surgery aims to restore function, enhance appearance, and ensure the health and safety of the eyes while improving the overall facial aesthetics.

Benefits of oculoplastic surgery

Oculoplastic surgery provides significant benefits by improving both function and appearance around the eyes. It can correct vision problems caused by droopy eyelids and repair tear duct blockages or orbital fractures. Aesthetic enhancements include reducing wrinkles, removing excess skin, and rejuvenating the eye area for a more youthful look.

Improved Vision

Enhanced Appearance
