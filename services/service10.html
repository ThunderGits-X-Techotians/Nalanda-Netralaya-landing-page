About our laser d.c.r

At Nalanda Netralaya, Laser Dacryocystorhinostomy (Laser DCR) is a minimally invasive procedure used to treat blocked tear ducts. This advanced technique utilizes laser technology to create a new passage between the tear sac and the nasal cavity, allowing tears to drain properly. Unlike traditional DCR, Laser DCR is performed without external incisions, resulting in no visible scars and a quicker recovery time. It is an effective and less invasive alternative for patients experiencing excessive tearing and recurrent infections due to tear duct obstructions. about laser d.c.r

Laser Dacryocystorhinostomy (Laser DCR) is a minimally invasive procedure that treats blocked tear ducts using laser technology to create a new drainage passage between the tear sac and the nasal cavity. Performed endoscopically without external incisions, Laser DCR allows for faster recovery and avoids visible scarring. This advanced method effectively relieves symptoms of excessive tearing and recurrent infections, providing a more comfortable and cosmetically appealing alternative to traditional DCR surgery.

Benefits of laser d.c.r

High Accuracy

Laser DCT provides precise intraocular pressure (IOP) measurements by contouring to the eye's natural shape, unaffected by corneal properties like thickness.

Comfortable and Non-invasive

It offers a non-invasive, comfortable procedure with minimal discomfort for patients compared to traditional methods.

Consistency

The laser technology ensures highly consistent and repeatable results, improving glaucoma monitoring and management.

Corneal Independence

Laser DCT readings are less influenced by corneal irregularities, making it suitable for patients with corneal abnormalities.
