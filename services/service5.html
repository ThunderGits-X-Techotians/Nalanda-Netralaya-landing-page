about squint surgery

Squint surgery is a procedure designed to correct misalignment of the eyes, commonly known as squint or strabismus. In this condition, the eyes do not align properly, which can cause vision problems and affect appearance. During the surgery, our skilled ophthalmologists make precise adjustments to the muscles around the eyes to realign them. This procedure helps improve binocular vision (the ability to use both eyes together) and enhances the cosmetic appearance of the eyes. The surgery is typically performed under local anesthesia, ensuring a quick recovery with minimal discomfort. Our team at Nalanda Netralaya is dedicated to providing personalized care to help you achieve better vision and a more natural eye alignment.

Benefits of squint surgery

Squint surgery offers multiple advantages, including improved eye alignment and better binocular vision, which enhances depth perception and reduces eye strain. The procedure also provides a cosmetic boost by correcting visible misalignment, thereby increasing self-esteem. Additionally, it helps prevent potential future vision problems, contributing to an overall better quality of life.
