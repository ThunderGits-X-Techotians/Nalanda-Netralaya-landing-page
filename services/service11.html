About our glaucoma surgery

At Nalanda Netralaya, glaucoma surgery is offered as a specialized treatment to manage and reduce intraocular pressure (IOP), helping to prevent vision loss caused by glaucoma. The center provides a range of advanced surgical options tailored to the severity and type of glaucoma, including minimally invasive procedures like trabeculectomy, drainage implants, and laser surgeries such as Selective Laser Trabeculoplasty (SLT). These surgeries are designed to enhance or restore the eye's natural fluid drainage system, thereby lowering IOP. With skilled surgeons and state-of-the-art technology, Nalanda Netralaya ensures effective and personalized care for glaucoma patients, aiming to preserve their vision and improve their quality of life.

about glaucoma surgery

Glaucoma surgery is a treatment aimed at lowering intraocular pressure (IOP) to prevent damage to the optic nerve and stop vision loss caused by glaucoma. The surgery enhances the eye’s natural drainage system or creates new drainage pathways to help fluid exit the eye more efficiently. Procedures can range from traditional surgeries like trabeculectomy to minimally invasive options, including laser treatments like Selective Laser Trabeculoplasty (SLT). These surgeries are crucial for patients whose glaucoma cannot be controlled with medications alone, helping to preserve vision and prevent further damage.

Benefits of glaucoma surgery

Effective Intraocular Pressure (IOP) Reduction

Glaucoma surgery helps lower IOP, which is essential for preventing damage to the optic nerve and avoiding further vision loss.

Prevention of Vision Loss

By controlling IOP, surgery can halt or significantly slow down the progression of glaucoma, preserving the patient’s remaining vision.

Long-term Management

Surgical intervention offers a more permanent solution for maintaining IOP within safe limits, reducing the need for long-term medication reliance.

Improved Quality of Life

By stabilizing IOP and preventing further optic nerve damage, glaucoma surgery helps patients maintain their independence and overall quality of life.

Patient Care and Recovery

Our dedicated team ensures that patients receive personalized care before, during, and after treatment. We focus on educating patients about their heart health and providing support throughout their recovery journey.
