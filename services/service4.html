About Our microphaco service

At Nalanda Netralaya, we offer the latest in cataract surgery technology with MicroPhaco. This highly advanced procedure is a minimally invasive technique designed for more precise cataract removal, leading to quicker recovery times and superior visual outcomes.

about microphaco

MicroPhaco is an advanced form of cataract surgery that employs cutting-edge phacoemulsification technology to remove cataracts through tiny, minimally invasive incisions. The procedure uses high-frequency ultrasound to break the cloudy lens into microscopic fragments, which are then gently suctioned out of the eye. After the cataract is removed, an intraocular lens (IOL) is implanted, restoring clear vision.

Benefits of microphaco

MicroPhaco technology represents a breakthrough in cataract surgery, combining precision and innovation to deliver superior outcomes for patients. This advanced technique involves the use of high-frequency ultrasound energy to break up and remove cataracts through an ultra-small incision, typically less than 2mm. The smaller incision not only reduces trauma to the eye but also speeds up recovery time and minimizes postoperative complications.
