Phaco Surgery

Nalanda Netralaya offers advanced Phacoemulsification surgery for cataract treatment, ensuring quick recovery and precise results .

About Our phaco Surgery

At Nalanda Netralaya, we specialize in Phacoemulsification, commonly referred to as Phaco Surgery, a modern and minimally invasive technique for treating cataracts. Cataracts, which cause clouding of the eye's natural lens, are a leading cause of vision impairment, especially in older adults. With Phaco surgery, we restore clear vision and enhance the quality of life for our patients.

What is Phaco Surgery?

Phacoemulsification involves the use of ultrasonic technology to break down the cloudy lens (cataract) into tiny fragments, which are then gently suctioned out of the eye. The procedure requires a tiny incision, which means it is a minimally invasive surgery. After removing the cataract, an intraocular lens (IOL) is implanted to replace the natural lens, restoring sharp and clear vision.

Benefits of Phaco Surgery

Phaco surgery is a highly advanced technique for cataract treatment, offering numerous benefits for patients. From faster recovery to improved precision, this procedure helps restore vision safely and effectively.

Minimally Invasive

Performed through a tiny incision, leading to faster healing and minimal discomfort.

Quick Recovery

Most patients experience improved vision within 24 hours of surgery.

No Stitches Required

The incision heals naturally, without the need for stitches.

Improved Vision

Restores sharp, clear vision, improving quality of life.

Patient Care and Recovery

Our dedicated team ensures that patients receive personalized care before, during, and after treatment. We focus on educating patients about their heart health and providing support throughout their recovery journey.
