About our emergency eye care service

Nalanda Netralaya provides comprehensive emergency eye care services around the clock. Their team of skilled ophthalmologists is equipped to handle urgent and critical eye conditions, such as trauma, infections, and sudden vision loss. The facility is equipped with advanced diagnostic and treatment technologies to ensure rapid and effective care. Their goal is to deliver prompt, expert care to preserve vision and address any immediate eye health concerns.
