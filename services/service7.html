About our low vision service

At Nalanda Netralaya, our Low Vision Service is dedicated to providing comprehensive care and support for individuals experiencing significant vision loss. Our team of specialized ophthalmologists and vision rehabilitation experts work together to offer personalized assessments and tailored rehabilitation programs designed to improve functional abilities and enhance daily living.

about low vision

Low vision is a condition where an individual experiences a significant reduction in visual ability that cannot be fully corrected with standard glasses, contact lenses, or medical treatments. Unlike complete blindness, low vision allows for some degree of sight, but it often impairs the ability to perform essential daily activities such as reading, driving, or recognizing faces.

Benefits of low vision service

Low vision services offer numerous benefits for individuals with significant visual impairments, transforming their daily lives by enhancing functional independence and overall well-being. These services provide tailored solutions that enable patients to perform daily activities more effectively, despite their visual limitations. By utilizing specialized vision aids and devices, individuals can engage in tasks such as reading, writing, and navigating with greater ease and clarity. The incorporation of environmental modifications and personalized training further contributes to a safer and more manageable living environment, improving mobility and reducing the risk of accidents.
